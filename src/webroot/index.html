Welcome to the Erg Arcade!

The Erg Arcade makes training on any of the %concept2% ergometers - the indoor rower, the SkiErg or the BikeErg - much more enjoyable! Connect your ergometer to your computer and you can:

  • Use %tools% to improve your coaching and erging experience;
  • Watch groovy %visualisations% while you train; and
  • Play some awesome %games%!

And unlike a real arcade, all the applications on the %ergarcade% are free to use!

What is this, and why would I need it?

The %concept2% %performance-monitor% (PM) collects lots of information during an erg session which is useful in the context of training for improving health and fitness. However, spending lots of time on the ergometer can be, frankly, BORING. Applications from the %ergarcade% use the same information to provide a much more entertaining erging experience!

Quick setup

  1. Connect your %concept2% %performance-monitor% to your computer.
  2. Download and install the %c2proxy%.
  3. From the %c2proxy% widget in the system tray of your computer, click Launch browser.
  4. Choose from any of the %tools%, %visualisations% and %games% and start erging!

For more information, including setting up for multiple ergs, check out the %detailed-setup% guide.
