Author

Dean Fogarty is a software engineer, %concept2-coach% and CrossFit coach based in Brisbane, Australia. He came up with the %ergarcade% after deciding the data collected by the %concept2% %performance-monitor% could be presented in various forms that would help coaches and athletes in real-time.

The initial application was %omniscient%, a single web page application intended to present views of all athlete's performance monitors simultaneously and in real-time. Paired with a tablet, coaches were now able to coach individual athletes while watching the data from all athletes without leaving the floor.

Visualisations were next, starting with a port of %webgl-fluid-simulation% by Pavel Dobryakov. Experiments with the %unity% game engine led to ... and ... games.

Dean enjoys coaching the erg, training and coaching CrossFit, and writing visualisations for the %ergarcade%. He is very busy as a husband to Shannon and father to Thea, Ariel and Atlas.
