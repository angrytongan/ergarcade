Erg Arcade Applications

There are three types of applications available on the %ergarcade% - %tools%, %visualisations% and %games%. All of them utilise information generated by the %concept2% %performance-monitor% whilst rowing, skiing and biking. None of them interfere with any function of the %performance-monitor%, so you can use %ergarcade% applications at the same time as using apps like %ergdata%.

Visualisations

%Visualisations% allow the athlete to break the monotony of training while still remaining engaged during the workout. Visualisations are simple interactive movies that respond to your erging technique - cadence, pace, output and more are used differently for different visualisations to provide an entertaining unique experience.
