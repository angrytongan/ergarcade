Troubleshooting

%ergarcade% not working? Look for your issue below. If you are still having trouble, drop a line over at the %googlegroups% page or check out the %facebook% page.

%logo-link%

c2proxy

  • Why do I need to install the %c2proxy% on my computer?
    The %c2proxy% acts as the middle-man between your ergometer and computer. It translates the information from all connected ergometers and relays it to your web browser runinng an %ergarcade% tool, visualisation or game.
  • I've downloaded and installed the %c2proxy%. What now?
    The %c2proxy% icon will appear in your computers' system tray; click on that and a menu will appear. Click on Launch browser and choose from the list of %tools%, %visualisations% or %games%.
  • I have a Windows computer. Does %ergarcade% work on it?
    Windows support for %c2proxy% is on the %roadmap% and will be completed soon.
%logo-bicycle%

Ergometer

  • What ergometers can I use with %ergarcade%?
    The %concept2% indoor rowing ergometer, SkiErg and BikeErg are supported.
  • My ergometer doesn't have a square-ish USB connector. Can I use %ergarcade%?
    The PM4 and PM5 %performance-monitor%s are supported for use with %ergarcade% via USB cable only. PM1, PM2 and PM3 are not supported.
  • My gym cables has their ergometers connected to each other with cables. Can I use %ergarcade% in this environment?
    Your gym connects their machines in a RS485 network. This configuration is unsupported by the %c2proxy% but it is on the %roadmap%! Keep an eye on the %facebook%, %instagram% and %googlegroups% pages for updates.
