Troubleshooting Erg Arcade

%ergarcade% not working? Look for your issue below. If you are still having trouble, jump on the %wiki% or comment on the the %googlegroups% page.

%logo-general%

General questions

  • Does this work on my phone?
    %ergarcade% requires a computer that is connected to your ergometer(s) via USB cable. Once this is in place, you are able to use your mobile device as a %remote-viewer%. Be aware that the graphical nature of the %visualisations% and %games% on %ergarcade% will result in fast draining of your device's battery, and you may experience performance or rendering issues.
%logo-c2proxy%

c2proxy

  • Why do I need to install the %c2proxy% on my computer?
    The %c2proxy% acts as the middle-man between your ergometer and computer. It translates the information from all connected ergometers and relays it to your web browser running an %ergarcade% tool, visualisation or game.
  • I've downloaded and installed the %c2proxy%. What now?
    The %c2proxy% icon will appear in your computers' system tray; click on that and a menu will appear. Click on Launch browser and choose from the list of %tools%, %visualisations% or %games%.
  • I have a Windows computer. Does %ergarcade% work on it?
    Windows support for %c2proxy% is on the %roadmap% and will be completed soon.
%logo-bicycle%

Ergometer

  • What ergometers can I use with %ergarcade%?
    The %concept2% indoor rowing ergometer, SkiErg and BikeErg are supported.
  • My ergometer doesn't have a square-ish USB connector. Can I use %ergarcade%?
    The PM3, PM4 and PM5 %performance-monitor%s are supported for use with %ergarcade% via USB cable only. PM1 and PM2 are not supported.
  • My gym uses blue / yellow cables to connect all their ergometers. Can I use %ergarcade% in this environment?
    Your gym connects their machines in a RS485 network. This configuration is unsupported by the %c2proxy% but it is on the %roadmap%! Keep an eye on the %facebook%, %instagram% and %googlegroups% pages for updates.
%logo-gamepad%

Applications

  • Will all the applications work on my computer?
    All the %applications% on the %ergarcade% run in your browser. They have all been tested on the Google Chrome browser but should also work on Safari.
  • Some applications run slowly!
    Performance will vary depending on the specifications of your computer. Older machines will not run as well as more recent computers, and amount of RAM and CPU speed will also affect performance.

    Most applications will include settings for varying graphical quality or speed; consider changing these to lower settings and seeing if that improves performance.
