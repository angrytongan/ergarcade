The %ergarcade% is completely free for you to use in whatever setting you choose - at home, at the gym or in your rowing / skiing / biking studio! However, building, testing and delivering all the %tools%, %visualisations% and %games% takes time and resources. If you like the %ergarcade% and want to see more cool stuff, please consider becoming a patron and committing to a small monthly donation to fund development.

Patron benefits

As a patron you will:

  • Receive priority response to all application requests and bug reports;
  • Have a say in the development %roadmap%;
  • Be added to the beta test group to try new products before general release;
  • Have your name on the %supporters% page;
  • Have my thanks!

So go ahead - commit to becoming an %ergarcade% patron and contribute to making erging entertaining!
