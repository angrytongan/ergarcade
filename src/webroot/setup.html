Setup

%ergarcade% %applications% run in your %chrome-web-browser% and connect to the %concept2% %performance-monitor% on your ergometer either wirelessly over Bluetooth or cabled using USB.

Check the notes on each %application% for setup details.

Tips

  • Ensure your %chrome-web-browser% software is up-to-date.
  • Disable any screensavers or screen blanking.
  • For multi-erg setups, such as studios or gyms, connect all ergs to a powered multi-port USB hub, and the hub to your computer.
