Setup

%Applications% on the %ergarcade% connect your computer to your %concept2% %performance-monitor% ether via Bluetooth or over USB. In the case of Bluetooth connections, the data is collected directly by the application; for USB connections, an intermediate application called %c2proxy% must be installed on your computer.

Make sure to read the requirements and configuration notes on each application, as these detail the correct setup procedure and environment required.
