About Erg Arcade

Author

Dean Fogarty is a software engineer, %concept2-coach% and CrossFit coach based in Brisbane, Australia. He came up with the %ergarcade% after deciding the data collected by the %concept2% %performance-monitor% could be presented in various forms that would help coaches and athletes in real-time.

Dean enjoys coaching the erg, training and coaching CrossFit, and writing software for the %ergarcade%. He is very busy husband to Shannon and father to Thea, Ariel and Atlas.

History

The initial application was %omniscient%, a single web page application that shows a group of athletes performance monitors simultaneously and in real-time. Paired with a tablet, coaches were now able to coach individual athletes while watching data from the entire group, without leaving the gym floor.

Visualisations were next, starting with a port of %webgl-fluid-simulation% by Pavel Dobryakov. Experiments with the %unity% game engine led to this and that games.
