About the Erg Arcade

All applications in the %ergarcade% are %open-source-software%, and available for use at no cost in any setting. Please feel free to use this software to help your own training, improve your coaching or even setup your own ergometer studio or business. Go nuts.

I hope that you find something on the %ergarcade% which makes your training more fun! -Dean

Author

Dean Fogarty is a semi-retired software engineer based in Brisbane, Australia. After spending a significant amount of time training on the %concept2% rower, and becoming frustrated that there weren't any applications that made training engaging, entertaining or fun, he came up with the idea of the %ergarcade% as a way to improve the training experience.

When not sitting on the rower, Dean can be found on Reddit lurking in r/crossfit. Feel free to %df-reddit-contact him there%.

Custom works

If you are after a custom application, tool or visualisation that uses the %concept2% family of ergometers, and can't find it on the %ergarcade%, please %df-reddit-get in touch% and I'll see what I can do.
