Concept2 BikeErg Damper Chart

A chart showing pace, watts and calories per hour for various drag factors, as set through the flywheel damper, for the BikeErg.

Like all Concept2 ergometers, the BikeErg has an adjustable damper on that adjusts the amount of air entering the flywheel assembly, altering the drag factor in a way not unlike gearing on a bike. Below is an interactive chart that displays pace, watts and calories per hour for various drag factors.
